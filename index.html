The Vibe / FRAME 02

A community
built on play.

We're sober, social, and alive, building a St. Pete crew that shows up, plays hard, and actually remembers it.

Static Play started with a simple feeling: the joy of being a kid on a field day, picked for a team, racing your friends in the grass. No screens, no substances, just movement, laughter, and people who get it.

This is the first of many. Field days now, with pool parties, sound baths, and park yoga on the way. Come once and you'll see why people keep coming back.

The Day / FRAME 03

What goes
down.

Five hours outside, broken into teams, fueled by snacks and good energy. Here's the rough roll of the day.

01

Field Games

Relay races, tug of war, capture the flag, and the kind of made-up games that turn strangers into teammates.

02

Get Picked

You'll be drafted onto a team the second you arrive. Wear your color, find your people, play for pride.

03

Wellness Breaks

Stretch sessions and a reset moment between rounds. Move your body, catch your breath, hydrate, reset.
